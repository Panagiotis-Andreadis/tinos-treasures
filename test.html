Apartments

I. Apigania

Located on the upper floor, this seaside duplex apartment has a sea view, featuring a queen bed, two adult single beds, a marble-finished kitchen, a cozy living room with a flat-screen TV, and a sleek bathroom. An internal staircase connects the two levels. Step onto the veranda with its marble table and enjoy stunning views of Isternia village, the sea, and the island’s terraced landscape (xerolithies). Just a 2-minute level walk brings you to two beaches, two restaurants, and a quiet beach bar—no uphill climbs required.

The Location

Ormos Isternion is a car-free Cycladic cove where old-world Tinian charm meets understated luxury—quiet, welcoming, and effortlessly elegant.

Car-free seaside hamlet with an exclusive, resort-like feel
Home to one of the island’s most celebrated seaside restaurants
Cozy café–ouzeri serving traditional mezedes right by the water
Two beautiful beaches, one with a serene, understated beach bar
Wide waterfront promenade for children, families and friends
Everything a short, level walk away — no uphill climbs required
