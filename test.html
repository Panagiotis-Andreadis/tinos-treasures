The Location

Ormos Isternion is a charming blend of old-world Tinian tradition and understated luxury. This car-free seaside hamlet feels almost like a gated resort—quiet, welcoming, and effortlessly elegant. Home to the island’s most celebrated restaurant, a cozy café–ouzeri serving traditional mezedes, and two beautiful beaches (one with a subtle, serene beach bar), it also offers a spacious waterfront promenade perfect for children, families, and groups of friends to unwind. Everything you need is just a short stroll away, wrapped in the warmth and authenticity of true Tinos living.
