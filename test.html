Apartments

Apigania

Located on the upper floor, this seaside duplex apartment overlooks the sea, featuring a queen bed, two adult single beds, a marble-finished kitchen, a cozy living room with a flat-screen TV, and a sleek bathroom. An internal staircase connects the two levels. Step onto the veranda with its marble table and enjoy stunning views of Isternia village, the sea, and the island’s terraced landscape (xerolithies). Just a 2-minute level walk brings you to two beaches, two restaurants, and a quiet beach bar—no uphill climbs required.
